# Pricing

<br />

> Pricing is being finalized. For current plans and limits, see the pricing section on [veralithai.com](https://veralithai.com/#pricing). The details below are the billing model.

## Hosted (the dashboard)

When you send traces with [`veralith.log()`](/integration/log) or the [REST API](/reference/rest-api), evaluation runs on Veralith's backend and is billed against your project's **monthly trace quota**. The free tier includes **200 traces per month**.

Everything on the dashboard — the [judges](/getting-started/concepts#the-three-judges), [failure cells](/getting-started/concepts#the-six-failure-cells), [heal cards](/dashboard/heals), and [self-heal over MCP](/self-heal/overview) — is included; you're billed on trace volume, not features.

## Offline evaluation

[`veralith.evaluate()`](/integration/evaluate-offline) runs entirely on your machine against your **own** `OPENAI_API_KEY` — it does **not** touch your Veralith quota. It costs roughly **$0.005 per trace** in OpenAI usage. Good for CI gates and experiments.

## In short

| | Billed against | Cost |
| --- | --- | --- |
| `log()` / REST ingest | Your Veralith trace quota | Free tier: 200 traces/mo |
| `evaluate()` (offline) | Your OpenAI key | ~$0.005 / trace |
